# AutoShareSuite

**Simple one-click tool to share your music links, clips, and updates everywhere automatically.**

Made for independent artists who want to post to X, Instagram, TikTok, email lists, and more without doing every post by hand.

Type your message once, paste the link, hit the button — it handles the rest on your schedule.

## What it does right now (Version 1 - Strong starting point)
- Easy desktop window with big buttons and text boxes (GUI)
- Type your post and paste link
- Connect your X (Twitter) account once
- Send to X automatically
- Send the same link and message to your email list automatically
- Basic scheduling (send now or later)
- Remembers your accounts safely on your computer

This is the foundation. After "5 years of work" it becomes a full polished suite with AI help, analytics, more platforms, mobile app, and team features.

## Why this helps your music grow
One person can't post everywhere every day forever. This tool does the sharing work so your music gets seen by more real people who actually click and listen. Better than looping songs alone.

The goal after five years: A tool so good and easy that independent artists from places like Ohio can compete with bigger names by sharing smarter, not harder.

## How to use right now (Super simple)

1. Download or clone this repo.
2. Install Python if you don't have it.
3. Open terminal and run:
   pip install customtkinter tweepy schedule
4. Get your X API keys (free from developer.twitter.com - make an app and get keys).
5. Run the app: python main.py
6. In the app: Paste your keys once, type your post + link, hit Send or Schedule.

It will post to X and send a simple email (you set your email details once).

## Important notes
- Start with X and email. Other platforms need more setup or approval.
- Always make good content people want to see. The tool just delivers it.
- Never spam. Post useful or fun stuff.
- API keys stay on your computer only.
